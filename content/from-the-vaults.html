Here's an example ideal play to get into the extra turns:

  1. Land, cycle <%= link_card 'Glassdust Hulk' %>
  2. Land, <%= link_card 'Howling Mine' %>
  3. Land, hard cast Borderpost
  4. Land, <%= link_card 'Time Warp' %>

This deck definitely doesn't run itself, there's a lot of decisions to make. It is difficult to get into the combo extra turn routine, but once there, with a few <%= link_card 'Howling Mine' %>s out, you can discard enough artifacts into the graveyard that after time sieving an extra turn, playing <%= link_card 'Open the Vaults' %> to return them all to play (including 2 <%= link_card 'Glassdust Hulk' %>s), sac 2 sets of <%= link_card 'time sieve' %>s for 2 more turns then on the next turn <%= link_card 'Open the Vaults' %>, buffing your Hulks to 13/14s and swinging for lethal.

  • <%= link_card 'Path to Exile' %>: To get rid of <%= link_card 'Gaddock Teeg' %>
  • <%= link_card 'Ethersworn Canonist' %>: To stop elfballs
  • <%= link_card 'Negate' %>: For control/counter
  • <%= link_card 'Vedalken Outlander' %>: For blightning/red aggro
